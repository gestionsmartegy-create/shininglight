01 / Modeling

Dignity at the heart of modeling

At Shining Light Modeling, dignity is not just a value — it is our foundation.

We are committed to transforming the fashion and talent industry by putting dignity, purpose and inner beauty first.

We believe we are called to be a light in the world, leaving a lasting mark through fashion, model training, talent development and beauty.

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced model, we offer a platform that nurtures confidence, character and vocation.

Our mission goes far beyond the runway. Shining Light Modeling actively supports humanitarian causes, including fundraising for developing countries and raising awareness about healing childhood trauma.

“Dignity begins when you understand your values.”
— Claire Simada Aeschimann
What we offer
  • Professional modeling classes (walk, posture, photo)
  • Castings and runway preparation
  • “One Day as a Model” experiences
  • Shining Light Fashion Shows (Dakar, Saint-Louis, Kinshasa, Ottawa)
  • Career guidance and connections with designers
