A singular vision

Between science, fashion and giving

Shining Light was founded in 2020 by Claire Simada Aeschimann, who holds a master’s degree in experimental medicine and a bachelor’s in biotechnology. Passionate about research and driven by unwavering determination, she builds her path in medicine while leaving a remarkable mark on the worlds of fashion and philanthropy.

“Shining is not only about revealing yourself to the world. It is also about lighting the way for others — with dignity, heart and commitment.”

Modeling since the age of 17, Claire trained professionally in 2017 with Models International Management in Ottawa. She is also an interior decorator. Her journey has led her to collaborate with major companies and take part in prestigious events such as the Marie-Victorine fashion show, New York Fashion Week and Montreal Fashion Week. Her face also lights up advertising campaigns across the streets of Montreal.

From the very start of her career she faced barriers and stereotypes in her hometown. Those experiences strengthened her commitment to defending dignity, respect and inclusion in the modeling world. Confronted, directly and indirectly, with childhood trauma, she founded Shining Light Charity — aspiring to impact the youth of Africa and beyond, encouraging them to pursue their passion and explore their abilities without limits.
